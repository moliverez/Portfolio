Harvard University, Mazur Group

Global Social Network

  • Visual Design
  • Front End Development

The Mazur Group, a research team in the Division of Engineering and Applied Sciences and the Department of Physics at Harvard University, wanted to create a digital network for members of the Peer Instruction community to meet, connect, and share information online.

I worked with the Mazur Group to create and implement a visually appealing design theme for the PI network that established a consistent visual style, layout structure, and user experience. I was responsible for all phases of the visual design process including wireframes, style guide, mockups, and front-end coding.
