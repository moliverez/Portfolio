My Background

Education

I began my career as an educator. I have a Master’s in Education and have instructed learners of all ages, backgrounds and abilities. I've taught in formal classrooms, online environments, and professional conferences and workshops.

I have a natural inclination to teach and an ability to communicate complex information in easy to understand terms.

Design

Even before I knew what good design was, I recognized its power. I noticed certain “things” just communicated more effectively than others. A desire to understand the reasons behind this fueled my interest in design.

I’m constantly fascinated by the way all elements of design work together to make products accessible, relevant and beautiful.

Technology

My background in technology is fairly broad. I’m a self-taught front end developer, spent several years as a multimedia producer, and have experience providing a wide range of tech support.

Throughout my career I've combined technology and design to create simple, elegant solutions to complex problems.

My Skills

Learning

Design

I have special expertise in learning design. Much of my formal education and career has been spent designing, implementing and evaluating learning programs, products, and environments.

I’m particularly skilled in the design and delivery of online instruction, and in crafting creative solutions to interesting teaching and learning problems.

Web Design &

Development

I consider myself a practical designer. While I understand the theories and trends that guide the field, my primary concern while designing is simply “will this work?”- does this design communicate the information and feeling I’m trying to convey?

I also develop. Being able to combine the design and development phases allows me to take a very agile approach to production. While I love to design, development gives me the opportunity to flex both my creative and logical muscles.

User

Experience

My skills and interest in UI / UX go well beyond web design. Whether Im teaching, consulting, producing, or designing, my focus is always on creating environments and experiences that resonate emotionally with people.
